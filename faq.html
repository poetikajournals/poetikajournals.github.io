Do you ship internationally?
Currently, I only ship within the United States.
What is your return policy?
Since my journals are custom made and made-to-order, I do not offer returns. However, I am open to constructive feedback which you can leave via Etsy or by email.
Do you make all the journals at Poetika Journals?
Each vessel is meticulously and lovingly made by my hands alone, from beginning to end. Each signature is reduced in size by folding and cutting, and each text block is stitched together using waxed linen thread after being individually awl-punched. I also cut each piece of bookcloth, leather and book board myself, glue everything together, and add embellishments or pyrographed elements.
